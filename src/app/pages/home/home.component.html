The outbreak of the novel corona virus and its disease COVID-19 presented an unprecedented challenge for humanity. This pandemic changes all the aspects of our lives and will leave some scars in the post-pandemic world as well. At GSR, our focus is to lessen these scars with a dedicated commitment on three primary aspects: Healthcare, Education, and Employment.


We are social entrepreneurs creatively and relentlessly seeking solutions to the existing problems in our country and paving the way for a better and brighter India.

Our mission is to develop, build, and scale social solutions in ways that bring about truly revolutionary change. We emphasise not just on relieving the symptoms of a social problem but on finding ways to get to the root causes and address them, bringing about positive change on a grand scale in ways others can replicate.

We have set our sight in providing universal access to healthcare, imparting education and creating job opportunities

HEALTHCARE

Our goal is to offer reliable, affordable, and quality health care services to the marginalized communities that do not have access to these services.


EDUCATION

We strive to ensure that every child gets equal learning opportunities and quality education that makes the future generation self-reliant.

EMPLOYMENT

We want to make the youth of our country cross-skilled, employable, and self reliant.
