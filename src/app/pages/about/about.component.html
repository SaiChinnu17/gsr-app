Our Stroy: Rising Stronger Through Adversity

GSR Trust works towards building a new compassionate culture, with equality and inclusion as its foundation. We want to empower people and develop new ways to solve healthcare, education, and employment challenges aggravated in the post-pandemic era.

HEALTHCARE

Our goal is to offer reliable, affordable, and quality health care services to the marginalized communities that do not have access to these services.

Within days of the outbreak, - our founders who have cracked many healthcare challenges and worked extensively for social causes - were on the ground working with healthcare professionals, hospitals, and MedTech companies to do what ever it takes to lessen the impact of this adversity. Their extensive ground work helped them gather thoughtful real-time insights in to a crisis that will define an entire generation ahead.

Given the scale and fury of change during the pandemic and to mount an effective response, our founders actively engaged with other thought leaders, doctors, social groups, educators, professional networks and participated in collaborative sessions for ideas and solutions to adjust to unrelenting volatility.

The members skill fully captured lessons from their discussions and their own first-hand experience as a covid warrior. They gathered insights in areas like healthcare, education, and employability. As a country, we faced some success stories and setbacks; our founders decided to take each breakdown as a challenge and use it to create a breakthrough. The result is the GSRTrust.

OUR VISION

At GSR, the trust's vision is to create a sustainable, inclusive, equitable, and compassionate society that thrives and enriches people.


OUR MISSION

Our mission is to drive real social change toward inclusion and equity by focussing on three core areas of healthcare, education, and employment.
