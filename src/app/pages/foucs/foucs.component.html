HEALTHCARE

In healthcare, we want to adopt a holistic strategy. Therefore, our focus areas are establishing, developing, maintaining, and running dispensaries, laboratories, maternity homes, child welfare centers, old age homes, orphanages, homes for destitute women and differently-abled children, and providing financial assistance to the disadvantaged or weak for medical treatment.

The trust has veterans from the healthcare field who have worked at the ground level and area ware of the challenges, the necessary immediate steps, and long term goals that we need to address.


EDUCATION

Today's 21st-century education presents unique challenges and opportunities to students. We attempt to start a trail-blazing journey that uplifts education and makes it accessible to everyone. We are in pursuit of a transformed learning experience for our children.

We also want to focus on new and effective ways of imparting education. For this, we are in touch with educators, socialworkers, reformers, education institutes, and change makers. We also propose to establish, develop, maintain, and run educational institutions at all levels, including medical and technical institutions and universities/colleges / schools, and work for disseminating education and assisting academic research and developments.


EMPLOYABILITY AND UPSKILLING

Our goal is to create a world that works for all. We want to eradicate the lines of exclusivity and build an inclusive society. While inclusivity has many aspects to consider, we want to start by bringing in economic and social equality by creating equal opportunities and being self-reliant. For this, we are working with thought-leaders, social reformers, educators, and experts who can create opportunities, impart skills training and identify ways and means to upskill people and make them employable.


LETTER

Dear All, This year is a mixed bag of emotions. We celebrate the Amrut Mahotsav of India's independence while battling a deadly disease everyday. Our freedom, glorious 75 years of independence, is a hard-won gift given to us by our predecessors. Now we have the opportunity to create shared prosperity at an unprecedented level, especially after the pandemic.

We don't want to mention the way the pandemic has affected and changed our lives. We have been through it together. Each of us has been affected, each of us has witnessed, fought, and braved the situation and emerged stronger from it. Instead, we want to focus on the opportunity the pandemic has granted us to do something unique, reach out to others, bring a difference, and create an impact. Seizing this opportunity depends on us, you, me, and we. We have a chance that may perhaps not come again for another millennium: to craft a society that reflects our deepest values. In a world seemingly going in a million directions at once, we can, and must, choose a movement, a focus, and an intention. At no other time has it been so clear: our future is our choice.

Despite the unprecedented growth that our country has witnessed and our advances in medicine and technology, there are still thousands of people in our country who do not have access to quality healthcare, a good education, or the opportunity to participate in the institutions that will dramatically affect their lives and futures. However, these problem scan be surmounted if we fervently believe in it, if you and I join hands, if we come together and if we commit ourselves to this cause.

GSR Trust is our first step towards bringing in the change we wish to see. It is our tribute to our country and away of giving back to society. So join us as we embark on this committed journey to bring about a change. With your support and solidarity, we will rise stronger through this adversity.Jai Hind.
